Class 18, Monday


• Let's review a couple examples from the mid-term and chat about any next steps.

• First, finish your portrait coded in HTML and CSS.
• Next, on a piece of paper, draw out or note each animation you would like to implement.
• Remember that the animation should loop infinitely. Your animation can be very subtle (think hair blowing in the wind, eyes moving slightly), or if you prefer, more dramatic.

Oct. 21


Class 17, Wednesday – Mid Term!

Create and upload a new design for your home page using CSS.

Keep in mind the following considerations:
1. Remove all moodboards, and any pages with images gathered from the web.
2. Use at least one roll-over transition.
3. To review transitions, reference this resource: https://www.w3schools.com/css/css3_transitions.asp
4. Organize your projects by grouping links in a way that makes sense.
5. Make sure your project links are visible above the fold, so without any scrolling on the page.
6. Below your projects, create an About Me section. This is open to your interpretation and can contain text or shapes.
7. Do not use any jpgs on the home page. You can use text, shapes, and colors.
8. Infuse your personal style to your home page, but keep it legible and accessible.
9. Upload your design to GitHub before 6.40pm. It should be the "index.html" file on your GitHub account.
10. You are free to leave the class zoom after you complete and upload your page.
