Welcome to DES 350

Objectives & Goals.


This is an advanced graphic design studio course exploring interactive design.

Our goals in this course are:
1. Apply interaction design to advanced level projects.
2. Explore the combination of multiple tools and technologies of interactive design.
3. Creatively use CSS coding.
4. Structure and write Markup Language (HTML) and to style it by means of CSS.
5. Identify the separation of design and content. We will identify and utilize best practices in usability, accessibility, and digital user interface design.
6. We will enhance HTML pages with basic JavaScript.

Things I recommend for the class


In addition to the standard computer/laptop, I recommend the following:
- Adobe Dreamweaver, in addition to Illustrator and Photoshop.
- Server space on which to publish and share work. We will not be using a lot of space, so if you have a hosting service with your website, that will work great. If you do not have a place to share HTML files, we will go over in class on how to set up a free one.

Course policies. You are expected to


Work attentively and seriously. Maintain your concentration and focus as you work through all the exercises. There will be discussions and presentations during the class period. Arrive to class on time and stay for the entire class period.

Please be mindful and mute your Zoom unless you are speaking or presenting.

Do not copy work and pass it off as your own. This is a serious matter and can lead to a failing grade or worse. If you copy code from a site, please city it in your source code as you would in an essay.
